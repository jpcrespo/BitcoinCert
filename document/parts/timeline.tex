\section{Timeline}

The following timeline details the stages described in \ref{methodology} and is structured into four phases:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Weeks 1-4: Construction of the decentralized certification protocol using Taproot on Bitcoin.
    \item Weeks 5-6: Development of a Proof of Concept (PoC) and its deployment on the Bitcoin test network.
    \item Weeks 7-8: Evaluation of the performance and security of the implemented protocol.
    \item Weeks 9-10: Documentation of findings and publication of results.
\end{itemize}
