\section{Timeline}

The following timeline details the stages described in \ref{methodology} and is structured into four phases:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Weeks 1-7: Construction of the decentralized certification protocol using Taproot on Bitcoin.
    \item Weeks 6-12: Design of a Proof of Concept (PoC) on the Bitcoin test network.
    \item Weeks 13-14: Estimation of the performance and security of the designed protocol.
    \item Weeks 15-17: Wrinting findings and publication of results.
\end{itemize}
