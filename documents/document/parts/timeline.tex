\section{Timeline}

The following timeline outlines the execution plan of the project described in Section~\ref{methodology}.
It is structured into four sequential and partially overlapping phases, covering a total duration of seventeen weeks.

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Weeks 1--7}: Design and formal specification of the decentralized document verification protocol based on Taproot.
    \item \textbf{Weeks 6--12}: Proposal for a Proof of Concept (PoC) on a Bitcoin test network.
    \item \textbf{Weeks 13--14}: Performance and security evaluation of the proposed protocol.
    \item \textbf{Weeks 15--17}: Writing, revision, and preparation of the final thesis document and presentation.
\end{itemize}

The partial overlap between the first two phases reflects the iterative nature of protocol design and testing, allowing early prototyping to inform architectural decisions.
